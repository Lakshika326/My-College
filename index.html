About Us

Rippon Girls' College is a prestigious all-girls school located in Galle, Sri Lanka. Established in 1817, the school has a long history of providing quality education to young women. It offers a well-rounded curriculum that emphasizes academic excellence, extracurricular development, and moral values. With a focus on empowering girls through education, Rippon Girls' College fosters a supportive and inclusive environment that prepares students for future leadership roles. The school's beautiful campus and dedicated faculty contribute to its reputation as one of the top educational institutions in the region.
